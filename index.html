About me

I am an ambitious guy from the Dominican Republic. I'm a Telematic Engineer focused on coding, that's why I'm open to full-stack development opportunities. I studied my career at PUCMM where I got my first programming experience. Then upgraded my knowledge in some jobs in my local city. Recently I started Microverse to compact all that knowledge in a Full-stack development career.

I've worked for more than 5 years in programming-related jobs, many of them, like my work at Inverte, was very challenging for me. For that particular one, Inverte, I'm very proud of my work in creating a platform working with more than 2,000 active users. Many say that I am self-taught, but the truth is that I have learned from many people. Currently working with Node, Rails, PostgreSQL, React, Next.js, React Native, and Docker. I also have experience with Ruby and Rails. Sound interesting? Let's connect!
